From Simple English Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a complete list of cities in Ukraine. On 1 January, 2022, there were 461 cities (Ukrainian: місто, misto) in Ukraine. City status is given by the Verkhovna Rada, the Ukrainian parliament. The city status is only slightly related to the size of a populated place in Ukraine.

Smaller settlements are urban-type settlements (comparable to towns in English-speaking countries) and villages (Ukrainian: село, selo).

The list of cities is ordered by 2021 estimates of population and compared to the 2001 Ukrainian Census, except for Chernobyl which is an unofficial estimate. The cities with special status are shown in italic.
